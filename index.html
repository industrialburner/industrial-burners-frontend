Common Questions

FAQ

What is the warranty on your burners? +
All our burners come with a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and component failures. Extended warranty and Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) are available on request.
Do you ship across India? +
Yes, we supply and ship to all states across India. We have experience delivering to Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, Maharashtra, Gujarat, Rajasthan, and more. Delivery timelines depend on location and order size.
Do you provide installation support? +
Yes. Our trained engineers provide full on-site installation, commissioning, and initial testing. We also conduct a site inspection before installation to ensure the right setup for your process.
What fuel types do your burners support? +
Our burners support LPG, PNG (Natural Gas), and Diesel. Several models support dual-fuel operation. We also supply infrared catalytic burners that are smokeless and flameless for sensitive environments.
Are spare parts available in India? +
Yes. All critical spare parts — including igniters, solenoid valves, temperature controllers, pressure gauges, and ignition pins — are stocked in India for fast delivery and minimal downtime.
How do I choose the right burner for my application? +
Our technical team will help you select the right model based on your heat capacity requirement (Kcal/hr), fuel type, application (boiler, oven, dryer, etc.), and space constraints. Contact us via the form or WhatsApp for a free consultation.
What industries do you serve? +
We serve a wide range of industries including textile processing, food baking & drying, boiler heating, chemical & rubber, poultry & livestock, BBQ & commercial cooking, powder coating, and industrial conveyor systems.
How long does delivery take? +
Standard orders are typically dispatched within 3–7 working days. Custom or bulk orders may take longer. We will confirm the exact timeline after your order is placed.
Can I get a customized heating solution? +
Absolutely. We design bespoke thermal systems for unique industrial requirements. Share your process details and we will engineer a solution tailored to your specific heating needs and budget.
